Mission

As leaders of the Northeastern University chapter of the Association of Computing Machinery, our goal is to expose students to a diverse range of computer science topics, and build a community within the university. We believe that there's a lot more to computer science than what you see in the classroom, and we bring people together by hosting events that further their knowledge of the field.

Events

We have meetings every Wednesday at 19:00 during the academic year. We usually meet in 366 West Village H, which is located at 440 Huntington Avenue. Our past events include tech talks, workshops, coding competitions, and many others. Event dates and locations may change from week to week.

To find out more about our upcoming events, please check our facebook page. We use facebook for most of our event scheduling purposes, so it is updated very regularly. You can also sign up for our mailing list to receive event updates by email.

Contact

For general questions, comments, or event suggestions, the best way to reach us is by email at acm@ccs.neu.edu. We are always looking for speakers and event partners, so please contact us if you'd like to work together!
